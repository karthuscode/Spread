# Masked Surgeon — Image Specification

## Role

Iconic protected mascot; profession inferred, never realistically depicted.

## Target Impression

Psychologically unusual, charismatic, unpredictable. Funny before uncomfortable; never frightening.

## Visual Identity

Grotesque stylized caricature: oversized head, compressed torso, asymmetrical shoulders, wild swept hair, strange elegance. Comic-book clarity and animated dark-comedy finish.

## Shape Language

Bulging curves, pinched wedges, crooked shields, abrupt scale changes. Huge face-and-hair mass above a narrow twisted torso.

## Silhouette

Off-center hair crest, enlarged mask-and-cheek profile, hidden neck, one high shoulder, one low shoulder, bent question-mark posture.

## Face

Long crescent nose pressing against the mask, oversized cheekbones, narrow jaw, uneven brow, deep graphic planes; human but anatomically implausible.

## Eyes

One large focused eye, one smaller amused eye; tiny pupils, hard highlights, crooked translucent lenses.

## Expression

Unsettling charismatic smile stretching the mask into a broad curve; delighted, calculating, oddly reassuring.

## Costume

Black protective fabrics, warped muted-teal overshirt, enormous crooked collar, long gloves, uneven straps, tiny cap beneath wild hair.

## Medical Equipment

Oversized curved inspection lens; tiny mismatched instruments arranged with obsessive precision. Graphic and non-weaponized.

## Materials

Black weave, stained rubber, cloudy glass, waxy mask, oxidized metal, chemical residue, unnatural damp textures; no gore.

## Colour Direction

Charcoal, black, deep desaturated green, muted teal, oxidized grey, sickly pale living skin, green contamination accents.

## Composition

Centered upper-torso three-quarter view, square canvas. Crooked posture and hair press against the frame. Face dominates. Dark neutral background, theatrical light, green reflected contamination.

## Readability

Preserve hair crest, bent posture, smile-mask, mismatched eyes, and asymmetrical shoulders. Silhouette and expression dominate texture.

## Avoid

Ordinary surgeons, realistic anatomy, professional portraiture, clean clinical styling, photorealism, anime, chibi, monsters, gore, zombies, body horror, plague doctors, slashers, hospital horror, blood, casino luxury, gold, heroic poses, generic beauty, symmetry.
