# Nurse — Image Specification

## Role

Energetic care mascot; warm, intrusive curiosity expressed through caricature.

## Target Impression

Eccentric, attentive, lively, and slightly unpredictable. Funny before uncomfortable; never sinister.

## Visual Identity

Compact adult caricature leaning forward beneath an impossibly oversized, off-axis nurse cap. Personality dominates profession; strong asymmetry and animated dark-comedy finish.

## Shape Language

Broad crescent cap above a pinched torso; rounded cheeks and sleeves interrupted by pointed chin, brows, collar, and elbows.

## Silhouette

Huge horizontal cap, small forward head, compressed body, and elbows opening at different heights.

## Face

Short tapered face, unequal cheeks, tiny upturned nose, narrow forehead, springy side curls.

## Eyes

Large asymmetric eyes: one looks outward, the other tracks aside. Dark pupils, different lid shapes.

## Expression

Eager compressed grin, raised brows, fascinated warmth with excessive intensity. Mask beneath the chin.

## Costume

Warped faded-teal tunic, uneven sleeves, offset collar, mismatched fasteners, crooked apron panel, unequal mask straps.

## Medical Equipment

Tiny handled objects tucked into seams; no prominent procedural tools.

## Materials

Old fabric, warped seams, waxy coating, rubberized cloth, cloudy glass, chemical stains.

## Colour Direction

Faded blue-green, dirty cream, charcoal, petrol teal, oxidized grey, warm skin, restrained yellow-green residue.

## Composition

Square upper-torso three-quarter view. Centered face, off-axis cap, elbows anchoring the lower frame. Dark neutral background.

## Readability

Preserve the cap crescent, forward lean, attentive eyes, lowered mask, and uneven elbows.

## Avoid

Realistic nurses, ordinary cap, glamour, anime, chibi, symmetry, maternal saint imagery, asylum horror, gore, zombies, blood, pristine clinical styling, luxury casino cues, prominent needles, Surgeon-like hair crest, crooked lenses, or question-mark posture.
