Wild Substitution

The Virus Wild substitutes for every regular paying symbol, including the Masked Person.

The Masked Person remains immune to infection and cannot be transformed into a Wild symbol. However, Virus Wild symbols can still substitute for it during payline evaluation.

Example:

Masked Person – Wild – Wild – Masked Person – Tool

This combination is evaluated as four Masked Person symbols on the payline.
