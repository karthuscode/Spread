Symbols

The current version of the game contains the following symbol categories.

Human

Regular human symbols can be infected by the Virus Wild.

The game should include at least three different human designs to create more visual variety.

Masked Person

The Masked Person is a high-value symbol that is immune to infection.

It cannot be transformed into a Wild symbol by the Virus Wild.

Medical Equipment

Medical equipment symbols are currently planned as low-value symbols.

The game should include at least three different equipment designs to create more visual variety.

The exact equipment symbols have not been confirmed yet.

Symbols

The current version of Spread contains human, medical equipment, and special symbols.

High-Value Symbols

Masked Surgeon

The Masked Surgeon is currently planned as the highest-value regular symbol.

The character wears protective equipment correctly and is immune to the infection mechanic.

The Masked Surgeon:

* cannot be infected;
* cannot transform into a Wild;
* can still form winning combinations with Virus Wild symbols.

Nurse

The Nurse is the second-highest-value human symbol.

The character may visually wear a mask incorrectly, such as below the nose or around the mouth, explaining why the symbol is still vulnerable to infection.

The Nurse:

* can be infected;
* can transform into a Wild;
* is treated as a separate paying symbol.

Medium-Value Symbols

Human 2

Human 2 is a regular civilian character and a medium-value paying symbol.

The symbol can be infected by the Virus Wild.

Human 3

Human 3 is another regular civilian character.

It may have the same payout value as Human 2 while using a different visual design.

Human 2 and Human 3 are separate paying symbols and do not match each other on a payline.

Low-Value Symbols

Medical equipment symbols are planned as the low-value symbols of the game.

At least three different equipment symbols should be used for visual variety.

The exact medical equipment has not been confirmed yet.

Medical equipment symbols:

* cannot be infected;
* cannot transform into Wild symbols;
* form regular payline wins.

Special Symbol

Virus Wild

The Virus Wild is the main special symbol of the game.

It:

* substitutes for all regular paying symbols;
* can substitute for the Masked Surgeon;
* infects adjacent vulnerable human symbols;
* does not infect medical equipment or the Masked Surgeon;
* does not currently have its own payout.
